Hi, my name is Nick Chang.

I’m a writer, editor and content strategist.

I’ve spent a lot of time in Dallas, Maryland, upstate New York, North Carolina, Atlanta and Chicago. I’m motivated by learning new things, and my natural sense of curiosity is always leading me into something new.

If I could, I’d live in a Muji store and wear nothing but indigo-dyed fabrics. (I make a really good effort at the second one.) Few things bring me more joy than day hikes, milk tea and David Attenborough nature documentaries—but one of those things is the feeling I get when I put together just the right words in just the right order. Another thing is helping people do that for themselves, their company or their product.

Feel free to read any of my work or just say hi. I’m always looking for new and interesting opportunities.
